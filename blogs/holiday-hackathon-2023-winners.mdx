#### üéä Best "New Year, New Me" Hack 

**[New Year Resolution Generator](https://www.codedex.io/community/monthly-challenge/submission/wo8pui7ITPGVymSoPH2m)** - The Dawgs (@xaiss @barkley)

![Image](https://imgur.com/8k9eAOr.png)

**Website:** https://new-year-resolution-generator-app.vercel.app
**Tools:** JavaScript, Next.js, React, Prisma, Vercel
**Prize:** Apple Watches

The web app helps people create and store their New Year resolutions, generate new ones, and also provides a countdown timer to 2024. This team built and deployed the web app on Vercel in just 12 hours ‚Äî it was the fastest submission and overall just rock solid. Incredible job, Sebastian and James!

#### üíª Best Terminal App

**[Great Christmas Rescue](https://www.codedex.io/community/monthly-challenge/submission/4chxT7DPA9kGK3ahpT4A)** - faLALALALA (@choisauce, @hannahmackenzie01 @cedar_codes)

![The Great Christmas Rescue](https://i.imgur.com/QLYpTnS.gif)

**GitHub:** https://github.com/choisauce/codedex_holiday_hackathon
**Tools:** Python, `curses`
**Prize:** Apple AirPods

A text-based adventure game where the player has to go out and save Christmas from the evil Polar Bear Herbert! You will go around the North Pole completing various tasks and helping out everyone there! However, depending on what you do, you could either end up on the Naughty or Nice list! This is a team of second-time CS students and some of our favorite community members. Congrats Austin, Hannah, and Yazmine!
