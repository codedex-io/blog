## So here are some hackathons

If you are a college student:

- [HackMIT](https://hackmit.org) - Free hoodies (instead of a shirt!), connect with top tier companies, [vlog](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/8Zefn4Ggf7A)
- [Big Red Hacks](https://www.bigredhacks.com) (Cornell) - Lots of 1:1 time with employers, smooth logistics
- [HackUTD](https://hackutd.co) - Best experience, meet lots of cool people, fire merch designs, mascot plushies in claw machine

- [TreeHacks](https://www.treehacks.com) (Stanford) - Stacked resources (mentors, sponsors, and hardware), cool side activities
- [HackKU](https://hackku.org) - Friendliest organizers, friendly people, lots of food cuisines, lots of places to sleep (rest lounge included!)
