Victoria Kirst, better known online as vrk, is a tech veteran (former VP of Engineering at [Glitch](https://glitch.com), Tech Lead @ [Google](https://google.com), Software Engineer @ [The Browser Company](https://www.thebrowser.company) based in Brooklyn, NY.
