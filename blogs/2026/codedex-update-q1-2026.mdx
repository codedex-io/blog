## Daily Challenges

![Global Search GIF](https://i.imgur.com/SAssHWJ.gif)

## 📈 Subscriber Retention

![subscriber retention](https://i.imgur.com/32aNM1l.gif)

As of Q4, **~60% of Clubbers stay subscribed after 3 months.** To anchor this, Disney+ & Hulu bundle's 3 month retention is 80% and Netflix is 74%. 

EdTech tend to see much lower retention than media (Netflix) or traditional SaaS (Slack, Zoom, GitHub) but some of the most retentive products are games... classic MMO (WoW), live services (Roblox, Fornite), and mobile games (Clash Royal). So we know what the bar is.
