## Builds Editor v1.0

![Builds workspace](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/codedex-io.appspot.com/o/blogs%2F2026%2Fcodedex-builds-v1%2Fbuilds.gif?alt=media&token=1406b4c5-7492-41cc-b8dd-6c0f9138a969)
<Caption text="OMG. It's just so... beautiful. 😍" />

Meet your new online code editor... ✨**[Codédex Builds](https://codedex.io/builds)**✨.

Builds has been around for a bit, and now it's getting the love it deserves. Over the past few months, our Product team has been heads down rethinking the experience from the ground up. 

The goal was simple: 

> Build a free online code editor that's genuinely _delightful_ to use.

We've always believed the web should have a world-class coding playground. Editors like Glitch and OG Replit inspired countless technologists (ourselves included), and we wanted to help carry that spirit forward with an editor that's approachable, fun, and built for today's builders.

Here's what's new in Codédex Builds:

- 📁 **Multi-file projects**: Organize your code across multiple files and folders.
- 🖼️ **Asset uploads**: Add images, videos, audio, text files.
- ⚡ **Live preview**: Edits show up in the iframe instantly. No save + refresh dance.
- 🌐 **Public URLs**: Publish to `your-build.codedex.me` and share the link with anyone.
- 🎨 **Custom subdomains**: Pick your own slug or let us generate one from your Build title.
- 🧚 **Lumi**: Get AI-powered help while you build.
- 🔀 **Remix**: Fork any public Builds and make it your own.

Whether you're learning to code, prototyping your next idea or MVP, or sharing something you're proud of, Codédex Builds is ready for you. Go build something awesome. ✨
