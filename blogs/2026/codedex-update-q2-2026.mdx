## Builds Editor v1.0

![Builds workspace](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/codedex-io.appspot.com/o/blogs%2F2026%2Fcodedex-builds-v1%2Fbuilds.gif?alt=media&token=1406b4c5-7492-41cc-b8dd-6c0f9138a969)
<Caption text="I'm still heart-eyes over this. 😍" />

Meet your new online code editor... ✨**[Codédex Builds](https://codedex.io/builds)**✨.

Builds has been around for a bit, and now it's getting the love it deserves. Over the past few months, our Product team has been heads down rethinking the experience from the ground up. 

The goal was simple: 

> Build a free online code editor that's genuinely _delightful_ to use.

We've always believed the web should have a world-class coding playground. Editors like Glitch and OG Replit inspired countless technologists (ourselves included), and we wanted to help carry that spirit forward with an editor that's approachable, fun, and built for today's builders.

Here's what's new in Codédex Builds:

- 📁 **Multi-file projects**: Organize your code across multiple files and folders.
- 🖼️ **Asset uploads**: Add images, videos, audio, text files.
- ⚡ **Live preview**: Edits show up in the iframe instantly.
- 🌐 **Public URLs**: Publish to `your-build.codedex.me` and share the link with anyone.
- 🏷️ **Custom subdomains**: Pick your own slug or let us generate one from your Build title.
- 🧚 **Lumi**: Get help from your AI companion bot.
- 🔀 **Remix**: Fork any public Builds and make it your own.

Whether you're learning to code, prototyping your next idea or MVP, or sharing something you're proud of, Codédex Builds is ready for you. Go build something awesome. ✨

## Course Discovery

One of the most common questions we get is: _"I finished X course, what should I take next?"_

Until now, answering that question wasn't always obvious. So last quarter, we focused on improving course discovery, from the Course Catalog to the moment you complete a course.

### Course Catalog Updates

![course catalog](https://i.imgur.com/d7K3LRF.jpeg)
<Caption text="Jackie's Figma designs." />

We introduced 6 curated **Learning Journeys** to help you find the right path based on your goals instead of browsing course by course. Whether you're looking to become a SWE, dive into AI, or build your first game, there's now a guided roadmap waiting for you.

Our current Learning Journeys include:

- 🌐 Web Development
- 📊 Data Science
- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence
- 💾 Computer Science
- 🕹️ Game Development
- 🎨 Web Design

Each journey groups related courses together, making it easier to understand what to learn first, what comes next, and how everything fits together.

### End-of-Course Experience

![flow](https://github.com/codedex-io/blog/blob/main/blog-images/codedex-update-q2-2026/end-of-course.gif?raw=true)
<Caption text="This roadmap UI is so clean. 👌" />

Finishing a course shouldn't feel like reaching a dead end. 🎓

Jackie designed a brand new end-of-course UI that helps you seamlessly discover what to learn next. Instead of sending you back to browse the catalog yourself, we'll recommend the next logical step based on the course you just completed.

The goal is simple: keep your momentum going while making it easier to continue learning.

## Looking Ahead: A Brand New Chapter

We are officially entering a new era of Codédex. 🎺🎶

For the past four years, we've been in survival mode – trying to build something people love while making sure we'd still be around to build it tmr. Every week was a race against the clock. 

Now, for the first time ever... we have a little room to breathe.

Crossing 2 million learners and becoming profitable back to back are strong signals that what we're building is sustainable. It means we can spend less time worrying about revenue and more time obsessing over making Codédex better and thinking more long-term.

So Q3 is all about quality of our core product.

Better courses. Better features. Better experiences. More bug fixes. More audits. More polish.

If the first chapter of Codédex was about survival, this next chapter is about attempting to build the greatest tech education platform the world has ever seen and redefining what online learning and digital spaces can be. It's time to raise the bar higher and _be_ different. To push boundaries and pursue our vision unapologetically. We made it through the hardest part and it's time to _play_.

So... to everyone who joined us along the way:

Thank you for learning, practicing, building, and being part of our community.
Thank you for caring about human creativity.
Thank you for supporting our team. 
Thank you for believing in this dream that we’ve been working so hard to make a reality.

From the bottom of our hearts... thank you for everything. 🙏

See you next quarter.
