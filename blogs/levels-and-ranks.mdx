#### Where do I find my level and rank?

Your current level can be found in the stats section of your user profile. For example: [www.codedex.io/@sonny](https://www.codedex.io/@sonny).

Your rank is next to your profile picture in the badge form.

#### How did you come up with this system?

Before ideating our own, we dug into popular EdTech platforms like Duolingo and some of our favorite games, such as Dota 2, League of Legends, and Overwatch.

Everyone thinks about leveling differently, and so do we. After countless hours of researching and brainstorming, this system that we arrived at makes the most sense for our learner base and the neverending coding journey.

#### Can my level and rank go down?

While we didn't implement any rank decay mechanisms in CodÃ©dex, your XP can decrease if you reset your course progress in Account > Settings.
